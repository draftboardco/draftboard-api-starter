Manual paths

Ask someone with a relevant network (an investor, advisor, etc.) to send you their LinkedIn connections export. Drop the CSV here and any contact whose LinkedIn URL matches a target in your workspace shows up as an extra path on that target's drawer — same as Draftboard-derived paths, just sourced from a list instead of from the API.

The CSV stays on your laptop. It's stored in data.db (gitignored) and never uploaded anywhere.
