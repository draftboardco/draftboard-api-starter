Welcome to the Draftboard API Starter

A reference Flask app showing how to build on Draftboard's Integration API. Designed to fork and adapt.

What you can do with it

  • 1
    Find warm intro paths through your team's combined network into target prospects you care about.
  • 2
    Score each path by relationship strength so you know which one to ask first.
  • 3
    Track intro requests through the funnel from "asked" → "intro made" → "meeting booked".

{# ----- STEP 4: First sync ----- #} {% elif step == "4" %}

Run your first sync

Click Sync paths in the left sidebar to fetch your real targets and connections from Draftboard. The sample workspace clears automatically as soon as your real data lands.

What happens next

  • • Sync pulls your /targets list from Draftboard's API.
  • • Then per-target connections — usually a minute or two for a workspace with a few hundred targets.
  • • While syncing, the sidebar shows a live progress pill.
  • • When done, the sample data is replaced and you see your real workspace.
